Facebook Archive Download Instructions


Mark Zuckerberg has stated that,"By giving people the power to share, we're making the world more transparent." Yet Facebook as one of the world's most powerful platforms,lacks transparency in how much this platform breaches the privacy of all its users. Find out what they know about your profile.



1. Click at the top right of any Facebook page and select Settings.


2. Click download a copy of your Facebook data at the bottom of General Account Settings


3.Click Start My Archive
